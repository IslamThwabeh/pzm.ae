Best Used iPhone Under 1,500 AED in Dubai 2026: 5 Honest Picks

If your budget is under 1,500 AED and you want a real iPhone experience in Dubai 2026 — not a tired hand-me-down — the model and battery health you pick matter more than the marketing. Below are the 5 honest picks we recommend in our Al Barsha workshop, plus the models we tell people to skip even when the price looks great.

1. Pick #1: iPhone 13 128 GB (1,200 - 1,500 AED) — top of budget

The smartest spend in this range. Modern OLED, A15 chip, MagSafe, great battery life, still receiving major iOS updates for years to come. Aim for 85%+ battery health and original screen.

Best for: daily driver, students, professionals on a budget who want a phone that lasts 2-3 more years.

2. Pick #2: iPhone 12 128 GB (750 - 950 AED) — sweet spot value

Same flat-edge design language as the 15/16, OLED, 5G, MagSafe. Battery is the weak point on older units — insist on 85%+ or add 220 AED for a fresh battery service.

Best for: buyers who want a great phone for under 1,000 AED with the modern Apple look.

3. Pick #3: iPhone 11 128 GB (550 - 750 AED) — most popular budget pick

Still snappy with A13 chip, great cameras for the price, large battery cell. Older LCD screen and no 5G/MagSafe. iOS support runway is shorter now (2-3 more major iOS versions expected).

Best for: first iPhone, secondary phone, or a parent/kid handover device.

4. Pick #4: iPhone 12 mini 128 GB (650 - 850 AED) — niche but special

The smallest modern iPhone — OLED, 5G, all the 12-series features in a one-hand phone. Battery is small; only buy with 88%+ health or with a fresh battery.

Best for: small-hands users, anyone who hates today's giant phones.

5. Pick #5: iPhone 13 mini 128 GB (1,150 - 1,400 AED) — small + newer

If you want a small phone but with iPhone 13 software runway and better battery than the 12 mini, this is the best stretch buy in the small-phone category.

Best for: upgrade from 12 mini or SE without going large.

6. Avoid at this budget

  • iPhone XR / XS / X / 8 / 8 Plus: iOS support running out, battery cells usually tired.
  • Anything below 80% battery unless the seller already added a verified battery service.
  • iPhone SE 2020/2022 unless you specifically want the home button — the screen feels dated next to the 11/12.

7. The 4-point in-store check we run before handover

  1. IMEI check on Apple Check Coverage and Find My switched off.
  2. Battery health % visible in Settings → Battery (not "Important Battery Message").
  3. Display original (no greenish tint, no touch dead zones, no aftermarket dot pattern under polarized lens).
  4. Face ID / Touch ID + camera + speakers + ports all tested.

8. Buying safely

Always buy with a written warranty (we offer 30 days on every certified used iPhone), original Apple charger if possible, and a verified battery health number. If you also have an older phone to trade in, get a same-day quote on our sell page to lower the net cost.
