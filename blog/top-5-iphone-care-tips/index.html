Top 5 iPhone Care Tips for Dubai Buyers in 2026

A well-kept iPhone holds value longer, feels faster day to day, and is easier to trade when you decide to upgrade. These five checks are simple enough to do at home, but they also help our Al Barsha team understand the device quickly if you message us before visiting.

1. Keep battery health visible in your decisions

Battery health is one of the first numbers buyers ask about. Open Settings, check maximum capacity, and watch whether the phone drops quickly during maps, video calls, or gaming. If the number is low, compare the care cost against the value of upgrading before making a decision.

2. Use the right charger and cable

Unstable charging causes more daily frustration than most cosmetic scratches. Use quality cables, avoid loose adapters, and check the port for dust if charging starts and stops. If the cable only works at one angle, get the phone checked before the port gets worse.

3. Protect the screen before the first drop

A clean screen protector and properly fitted case are cheaper than a damaged display. If the screen already has lines, touch dead zones, or brightness patches, send us a clear photo on WhatsApp before visiting so we can guide you on the right next step.

4. Free storage before performance feels slow

iPhones with full storage can feel older than they are. Keep at least 10 to 15 percent free, remove duplicate videos, and back up important photos before major iOS updates. This also makes trade-in preparation easier when you are ready to reset the device.

5. Check value before upgrading

Before buying a new iPhone, check the value of your current model. Battery health, storage, box availability, and screen condition all affect the quote. Our sell devices page explains what to send for a fast trade-in estimate.
