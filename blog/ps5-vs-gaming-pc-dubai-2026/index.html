PS5 vs Gaming PC in Dubai 2026: Honest AED Math by Use Case

"PS5 or build a PC?" is the most common gaming question we get from Al Barsha customers in 2026. Most online comparisons stop at the sticker price. The real answer in Dubai is the 3-year total cost — and that flips the winner for most use cases.

1. Upfront AED prices (May 2026)

  • PS5 Slim (disc): 1,950 - 2,200 AED
  • PS5 Pro: 2,900 - 3,400 AED
  • 1080p custom gaming PC: 3,400 - 4,200 AED
  • 1440p custom gaming PC (sweet spot): 5,800 - 7,200 AED

On the sticker, the PS5 wins. But you do not pay the sticker price — you pay the 3-year total.

2. The 3-year total — PS5 Slim (typical Dubai buyer)

  • Console: 2,000 AED
  • 2 extra controllers: 750 AED
  • PS Plus Extra (3 years, ~370 AED/year): 1,110 AED
  • 8-10 full-price games at 200-280 AED each: 1,800-2,400 AED
  • Optional 1 TB SSD upgrade: 350 AED
  • Total: ~5,200 - 6,000 AED

3. The 3-year total — 1440p custom gaming PC

  • PC build: 6,800 AED
  • Keyboard + mouse + headset: 350 AED
  • Game Pass Ultimate (3 years, ~60 AED/month): ~2,160 AED — OR Steam sales averaging 30-50% off most titles
  • Online play: free
  • Storage upgrade: 0 AED (build already 1 TB Gen4)
  • Total: ~7,000 - 9,300 AED (Game Pass path) or ~7,500 - 8,500 AED with Steam sales

The PC is 1,500-3,000 AED more across 3 years — but you get 1440p high settings, mods, emulation, productivity, and the ability to upgrade only the GPU at year 4 instead of buying a whole new console at year 5.

4. Choose PS5 if

  • Sony first-party exclusives day-one matter to you (Spider-Man, God of War, GT, Last of Us).
  • You play on a TV with the family — couch co-op, controllers, simple setup.
  • You do not want to think about settings, drivers, or RAM speeds.
  • Online multiplayer with friends who are also on PlayStation.

5. Choose Gaming PC if

  • You want 1440p or 4K at 100+ FPS with the freedom to lower or raise settings.
  • Free online play and Game Pass / Steam sales matter to your wallet.
  • You also work, code, edit, or stream from the same machine.
  • Mods, emulation, or competitive shooters (CS2, Valorant, R6) are on your list.
  • You plan to upgrade just the GPU in 3-4 years instead of buying a new device.

6. The hybrid that wins surprisingly often

Buy a PS5 Slim for exclusives and couch sessions, and a 1080p custom PC at 3,400-4,200 AED for everything else. Total entry: ~5,500-6,500 AED — about the same as a Pro+games combo, but you cover both libraries.

7. Resale value in Dubai

A 3-year-old PS5 Slim resells for 800-1,100 AED (~50% loss). A 3-year-old 1440p PC resells well in parts — the case, PSU, RAM, and storage keep value; the GPU is the depreciation item, and it is the only part you would replace.
