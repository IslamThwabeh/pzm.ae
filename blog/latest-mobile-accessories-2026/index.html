Must-Have Phone and Laptop Accessories in 2026

The right accessories do not just protect your devices — they transform how you use them. Here are the accessories worth your money in 2026, from essential protection to productivity boosters, all available at tech stores across Dubai.

1. Screen protectors: tempered glass is non-negotiable

If you have spent thousands on a new iPhone 17 or flagship Samsung, a premium tempered glass protector is the cheapest insurance you can buy. Look for 9H hardness rating, oleophobic coating (anti-fingerprint), and edge-to-edge coverage. Privacy screen protectors are worth considering if you use your phone for banking or work emails in crowded places like the Metro. Browse our full accessories selection for all major phone models.

2. Cases that actually protect

Clear cases look great for a month, then yellow. In 2026, the move is toward matte or frosted cases with MagSafe compatibility — they stay clean, offer military-grade drop protection, and work with magnetic chargers and car mounts. For iPhones, Apple's own silicone and FineWoven cases remain top picks. For Samsung, Spigen and UAG offer the best protection-to-bulk ratio. A strong case and protector combo is still much cheaper than most screen or frame repairs after a bad drop.

3. GaN chargers: smaller, faster, multi-device

GaN (gallium nitride) chargers have essentially replaced traditional brick chargers. A single 65W GaN charger can fast-charge your iPhone, iPad, and MacBook from one compact unit — perfect for travel and desk organization. For gaming laptop owners, 100W+ GaN chargers now exist that can handle even power-hungry machines. Always buy from reputable brands — cheap chargers can damage your battery or worse.

4. Wireless charging pads and stands

MagSafe and Qi2 chargers have matured significantly. A 3-in-1 charging stand (phone + watch + earbuds) eliminates cable clutter on your nightstand. For desk setups, a MagSafe-compatible stand keeps your phone at eye level for video calls and notifications. These make excellent gifts and everyday upgrades.

5. Laptop accessories that boost productivity

  • USB-C hubs: Essential for MacBook users — especially if you picked up a recent used MacBook or slim Windows laptop that dropped legacy ports. Get one with HDMI, USB-A, SD card, and passthrough charging.
  • Laptop stands: Elevating your screen to eye level reduces neck strain and improves airflow for cooling.
  • External keyboards and mice: If you use your laptop at a desk, a proper keyboard and mouse transform the experience.
  • Portable SSDs: Samsung T7 Shield or SanDisk Extreme — fast, rugged, and perfect for backups and file transfers.
