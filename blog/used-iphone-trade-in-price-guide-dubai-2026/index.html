How Much Is My Used iPhone Worth in Dubai? Trade-In Price Guide 2026

"How much will you give me for my iPhone?" is the most common WhatsApp message we receive. The honest answer depends on five things — and most buyers in Dubai overestimate two of them and underestimate three. Below are realistic 2026 AED ranges and the exact checks that move the number up or down before you walk into our Al Barsha store.

1. Realistic AED trade-in ranges (May 2026, 128/256 GB, great condition, 88%+ battery)

  • iPhone 16 Pro Max: 3,200 - 4,100 AED
  • iPhone 16 Pro: 2,700 - 3,400 AED
  • iPhone 16 / 16 Plus: 1,900 - 2,600 AED
  • iPhone 15 Pro Max: 2,400 - 3,100 AED
  • iPhone 15 Pro: 2,000 - 2,600 AED
  • iPhone 15 / 15 Plus: 1,400 - 1,900 AED
  • iPhone 14 Pro Max: 1,700 - 2,200 AED
  • iPhone 14 / 14 Plus: 1,000 - 1,400 AED
  • iPhone 13 Pro Max: 1,200 - 1,650 AED
  • iPhone 13 / 13 mini: 700 - 1,000 AED
  • iPhone 12 series: 450 - 850 AED
  • iPhone 11 series: 300 - 600 AED

Ranges shift weekly based on supply, dollar moves, and the iPhone 17 trade-in wave. Pro Max trade-ins hold strongest. Always confirm via our trade-in service before deciding.

2. What raises your quote (most people undervalue these)

  • Battery health 90%+: can add 100-250 AED.
  • Original box + cable: adds 80-150 AED on Pro models.
  • All-Apple repair history: if any past repairs used original Apple parts.
  • Pristine back glass: Pro models with no micro-scratches qualify for top-tier offer bracket.
  • UAE-bought unit: Saudi/EU variants are accepted but priced slightly lower because of warranty markets.

3. What kills your quote (most people overlook these)

  • Third-party screen replacement: -250 to -600 AED.
  • Face ID partially broken or dot projector flagged: -400 to -900 AED on Pro models.
  • iCloud lock / Find My on: trade-in cannot complete until removed.
  • Battery health below 80%: phone is rebatteried before resale — cost passes through to your quote.
  • Significant frame dents or bent chassis: -300 to -800 AED depending on severity.
  • Service Genuine Parts warning in Settings → General → About: reduces quote and may require disclosure to next buyer.

4. The 5-minute WhatsApp valuation checklist

Send these in one message for a same-day quote:

  1. Model + storage (e.g., 15 Pro Max 256 GB).
  2. Battery health % (Settings → Battery → Battery Health).
  3. Region purchased (UAE / KSA / other).
  4. Original box and cable yes/no.
  5. 2 clear photos: front (screen on) + back.

You will get a price band, not a single number — that is honest. The final in-store offer after a 15-minute check usually lands within 5% of the band.

5. Should you trade in now or wait?

If your iPhone is 16 Pro Max or 15 Pro Max — trade now. The 17 launch cycle is still pulling values up. If your iPhone is 12, 11, or older — values are already at the floor; you are not losing much by waiting a month, but you are also not gaining. For everyone in between, the smart move is to lock the quote, upgrade, and not look at depreciation graphs.
