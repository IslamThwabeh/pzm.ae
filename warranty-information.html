Warranty Information

Last updated: May 26, 2026

This page summarizes warranty terms for devices sold by PZM Computers & Phones Store in Al Barsha, Dubai.

1. New Devices

New devices are sold with applicable manufacturer warranty terms. Coverage period and conditions depend on the product brand and model.

2. Pre-owned Devices

Pre-owned devices include store warranty coverage as disclosed at the point of sale. Coverage details are confirmed before payment.

3. What Warranty Does Not Cover

  • Physical damage after handover
  • Liquid damage caused after purchase
  • Unauthorized modification or tampering
  • Damage caused by misuse, drops, or power surges

4. Warranty Claim Process

  • Contact the store with your purchase details
  • Bring the device for in-store assessment
  • Follow the confirmed warranty path provided by the team
