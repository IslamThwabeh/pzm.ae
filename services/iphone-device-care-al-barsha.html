What Can We Help With?

We assess the device first, explain the available route and price, and wait for your approval before paid technical work begins.

Screen Replacement

Support for cracked glass, damaged displays, touch-response problems, screen lines, flickering and blank screens.

Battery Replacement

Assessment for rapid battery drain, unexpected shutdowns, overheating, charging problems and reduced battery capacity.

Charging Port Service

Inspection and service for loose connections, intermittent charging, blocked ports and devices that no longer charge normally.

Camera Service

Support for blurry images, camera shaking, focus problems, dark camera views and damaged camera glass.

Speaker and Microphone Service

Assessment for low sound, distorted audio, unclear calls, microphone problems and blocked speaker openings.

Careful assessment following liquid exposure. Outcomes cannot be guaranteed because corrosion and internal damage may develop over time.

Software and Performance Support

Help with update problems, storage pressure, slow performance, backup preparation and device setup.

Component Options and Completion Times

Choose What Fits Your Device and Budget

Available component grades vary by model and current stock. Where multiple options exist, we explain differences in display quality, performance, expected durability, price and warranty before you decide.

The selected component type is recorded on your service receipt. A component is not described as manufacturer-supplied unless its source can be confirmed.

Same-Day Service When Possible

Many common screen, battery, charging, camera and audio services can be completed on the same day when the required component is in stock.

Complex internal work, liquid exposure and uncommon models generally require 24–48 hours. Special-order components can take longer. Timing is confirmed before work begins.

30-Day Limited Service Warranty

What It Covers

  • The component supplied by PZM
  • Workmanship directly related to the completed service
  • Recurrence of the same confirmed issue during the coverage period

What It Does Not Cover

  • Drops, impact, pressure, misuse or new liquid exposure
  • New or unrelated faults
  • Software, data, password or account issues
  • Normal reduction in battery capacity
  • Work performed or components changed elsewhere afterward
  • Previously disclosed internal, motherboard or liquid-related damage

Liquid-exposed devices receive coverage only when it is specifically confirmed in writing on the service receipt.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the initial iPhone assessment free?

Yes. The initial assessment is free. If deeper technical investigation may involve a charge, it will be explained before proceeding.

Can my iPhone be completed on the same day?

Many common services can be completed on the same day when the required component is available. Complex issues generally require 24–48 hours.

What component options are available?

Options depend on the model and current availability. We explain the component grade, expected performance, price and warranty before you approve it.

Is there a warranty?

Yes. Completed technical services include a 30-day limited warranty covering the supplied component and associated workmanship, subject to the exclusions above.

Can you help with a water-exposed iPhone?

Yes, we can assess it. Liquid exposure can cause progressive internal damage, so successful recovery and long-term performance cannot be guaranteed.

Do I need an appointment?

Walk-ins are welcome. Contacting us through WhatsApp first is recommended so we can check likely component availability.

Is PZM authorized by Apple?

No. PZM is an independent device-care provider and is not affiliated with or authorized by Apple.

Should I back up my iPhone first?

Yes, whenever possible. A current backup protects important information if the device has existing internal or software damage.
