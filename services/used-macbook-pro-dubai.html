Scenario fit: when Pro is right (and when it is not)

  • Best fit for creators, developers, and power users who need sustained performance under heavier workloads.
  • Often not ideal for light browsing-only use where battery-first value may matter more than peak performance.
  • If your workload is moderate, compare MacBook Air for lighter daily use and student-focused budget laptop options.

If your apps stay open all day and performance stability is your priority, this Pro path is usually the better route. If your use is lighter, the Air route can be better value.
