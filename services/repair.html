Repair Questions Before You Visit

Can I message you before coming to the store?

Yes. Send the device model and issue on WhatsApp before you visit and we will help you understand the likely next step, timing, and whether store drop-off is the best option.

Do you confirm the repair price before starting?

Yes. We inspect the device, explain the issue, and share the repair quote before work starts so you can approve the next step clearly.

Are some repairs completed the same day?

Yes. Common repairs can sometimes be completed the same day when the issue, required parts, and workload allow. More complex work takes longer after diagnosis.

Do you repair MacBook and laptop issues too?

Yes. PZM handles MacBook and laptop issues such as screens, keyboards, power faults, storage upgrades, cooling problems, and general diagnostics. If you are visiting from another area, see our pages for Al Barsha, Tecom, or Dubai Marina.
