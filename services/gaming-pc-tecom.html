Why build locally instead of ordering online?

Dubai parts pricing moves fast, and online listings often hide weak PSUs, slow RAM, or aftermarket coolers that throttle. A local build at PZM means proper part selection, real cable management for airflow in summer, thermal paste applied correctly, and a stress test before you pay the final.
