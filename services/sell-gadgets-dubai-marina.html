Sell-From-Dubai-Marina Questions

Do you collect from Dubai Marina or do I visit the store?

Most sellers ride to the Al Barsha store on Hessa Street because it keeps the deal simple and lets you see the inspection. Some larger trade-ins can be arranged differently — confirm on WhatsApp first.

How long does the sell visit take from Dubai Marina?

Once we have already agreed a price range on WhatsApp, the in-store check usually takes 10–20 minutes for phones and tablets, and a bit longer for MacBooks and gaming consoles.

What gets the best price from Dubai Marina sellers?

Devices in clean condition with strong battery health, box and accessories included, and no activation lock or pending finance always quote highest. Send photos and battery health first.

Can I trade in toward a new iPhone instead of cash?

Yes. Tell us your preferred next device on WhatsApp and we will quote both the straight cash number and the trade-in number toward a new or tested used iPhone.
