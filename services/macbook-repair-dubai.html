MacBook Repair Dubai

Screen, Battery, Keyboard, Liquid Damage & Board-Level Repair

PZM in Al Barsha handles MacBook repairs across Dubai, covering everything from battery replacements and screen swaps to liquid damage assessments and board-level diagnostics. Before any work begins, we inspect the MacBook, explain the repair path, and confirm the quote so you always approve the next step.

Common MacBook repairs at PZM

  • Battery replacement — MacBook Air and MacBook Pro, M1 through M3 and Intel models
  • Screen replacement — cracked, flickering, or dead display assemblies
  • Keyboard repair — sticky, unresponsive, or physically damaged keys and top cases
  • Liquid damage — board inspection, corrosion treatment, component-level assessment
  • Charging port repair — MagSafe and USB-C charging faults
  • Performance issues — slow boot, overheating, fan noise, and storage faults

Why message before visiting

The fastest repair route starts with a WhatsApp message. Send the exact MacBook model (found on the back or in About This Mac), describe the fault clearly, and mention whether the device powers on. This lets us check parts availability and give you a realistic timing estimate before you travel to Al Barsha from anywhere in Dubai.

Repair or replace?

If the repair cost is high relative to the MacBook's age, we can help you compare the quote against tested used MacBooks in stock. A used MacBook Air M2 in excellent condition is often a stronger investment than an expensive logic board repair on a 2018 Intel model. We give you the comparison before you decide — not after.

What to send before visiting

  • MacBook model name and year (or serial number)
  • Clear description of the fault — what is broken, what symptoms you see
  • Whether liquid damage occurred and when
  • Whether the MacBook powers on

MacBook Repair Questions for Dubai

What MacBook models do you repair in Dubai?

PZM repairs MacBook Air (M1, M2, M3, Intel), MacBook Pro (13", 14", 16", Intel and Apple Silicon), and older Intel models. Send your model or serial number on WhatsApp before visiting for a faster quote.

How long does MacBook repair take?

Battery and keyboard repairs on common models are often same-day or next-day. Screen replacements and liquid damage repairs depend on parts availability and damage extent. WhatsApp the model and issue for a realistic estimate before you visit.

Do you give a quote before starting the repair?

Yes. We always inspect first, explain the repair path, and confirm the quote before any work begins. You approve the next step before we proceed.

Can you repair liquid-damaged MacBooks?

Yes. PZM handles board-level liquid damage assessments. Outcome depends on which components were affected and whether corrosion has spread. WhatsApp the model and damage description for an initial assessment.

Should I repair my MacBook or buy a used one instead?

It depends on the model age and the repair cost. PZM can compare the quote with tested used MacBook alternatives in stock so you make an informed decision before committing.
